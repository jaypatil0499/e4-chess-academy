Common Questions

Everything Parents Ask

What age can my child start?
+
We welcome children from age 5 onwards. Our Beginner programme is specifically designed for young learners with no prior chess experience — our coaches are trained to make learning fun and stress-free for this age group.
How does the free demo session work?
+
After booking, we schedule a 30-minute online session with one of our coaches. No preparation needed — the coach will assess your child's level, teach a short lesson, and answer all your questions. Zero commitment required.
Are sessions online or in-person?
+
All sessions are conducted online via our platform, or Zoom in some cases. This allows us to serve families across India and globally with the same quality coaching, from the comfort of your home.
What makes E4 coaches different?
+
Our coaches have strong competitive chess backgrounds and deep experience coaching children. They are not only skilled players but trained pedagogues who understand child psychology and learning patterns. Every coach undergoes our E4 Coaching Certification before teaching.
Can I switch plans or pause my subscription?
+
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or pause your plan at any time from your parent dashboard. We offer complete flexibility — no lock-in contracts, no cancellation fees.
Do you help with FIDE ratings and tournaments?
+
Yes — our Intermediate and Advanced courses include dedicated tournament preparation. We help students register for FIDE-rated events, prepare opening repertoires specific to upcoming tournaments, and provide psychological coaching for competitive play.
